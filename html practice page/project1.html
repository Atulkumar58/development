linus Torvalds

The Creator of Linux

Biography

is a Finnish-American software engineer who is the creator and lead developer of the Linux kernel. He also created the distributed version control system Git

Achievements

  • 1991: created the Linux Kernel and the version control system Git
  • 2004: Named one of the "100 Most influencial People in the World" by Time magazine in 2004
  • 2010: Received an honourary doctorate from the University of Helsinki in 2010
  • 2013: Inducted into the Internet Hall of Fame in 2013 for his contributions to the decelopment of the Linux kernel and the creation of Git
  • 2014: Inducted into theHall of Fame of the computer pioneer Award
  • 2015: Recieved the 2015 IEEE Computer Society Computer Pioneer Award
  • 2019: Recieved the 2019 ACM SIGOPS Hall of Fame Award
